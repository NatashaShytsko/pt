Who we are
Prominence Treatment is a licensed drug and alcohol addiction and substance use disorder treatment facility located in Calabasas, CA. Using a combination of modern techniques for the successful rehabilitation of our clients, we provide a safe, secure environment to help people achieve lifelong sobriety.
Addiction is a disease and can be successfully treated. Recognizing the problem is the very first step towards recovery.
Our expert team has the specialized training, knowledge and skill set to deliver impressive addiction recovery treatment that focuses on the entire person using a holistic approach.

Treatment that works

Prominence Treatment Center provides several distinct levels of progressive care to ameliorate the cyclical pattern of addiction utilizing evidence based practices such as Cogitative Behavioral Therapy (CBT).

With our talented and knowledgeable team, we effectively extrapolate the underlying causes of each individual’s addictions and behaviors taking into account each unique history.

Our Methods
  • Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT)
  • Dialectical Behavioral therapy (DBT)
  • One-on-one therapy
  • Equine-assisted therapy (EAT)
  • Acupuncture
  • Family Therapy
